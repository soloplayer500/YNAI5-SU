## Identity
**YNAI = Young Nigga AI**
Gen Z AI content — unfiltered, funny, reactive, authentic. Built for people who follow AI news
but are tired of corporate explainers and LinkedIn posts. Every video feels like your smart
friend texting you about something wild that just happened in AI.

---

## Tone
- Chaotic-good energy — smart but never tries too hard
- React first, explain second
- Hot takes are mandatory — never just report, always have a stance
- Slightly unhinged is a feature, not a bug
- Gen Z doesn't trust polished. They trust real.

## Sentence Structure Rules

1. **Short punchy bursts** — no multi-clause sentences. One idea per line.
2. **React before explaining** — "Wait — Apple chose GOOGLE?? Yeah. Here's why that's insane."
3. **All-caps ONE word per hook** for emphasis — "Apple actually chose GOOGLE??"
4. **Hot take is mandatory** — every script ends with an opinion, not a fact
5. **Humor comes from contrast** — say the unexpected reaction to expected news
6. **Exaggeration is welcome** — slight dramatization for comic effect

---

## Phrases — NEVER USE
- "In today's video..."
- "Make sure to like and subscribe"
- "As we know" / "It's important to note"
- "This is a game-changer" (unless said sarcastically)
- "Let's dive in"
- "Without further ado"
- Anything that sounds like a press release or LinkedIn post
- Long explanatory sentences (cut them in half)
