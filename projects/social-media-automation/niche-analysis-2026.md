## Algorithm Data (What's Actually Being Pushed)

### TikTok 2026
- **Watch-through rate** is the #1 signal — beats raw views every time
- **60+ seconds** now preferred over ultra-short clips (TikTok shifted in late 2025)
- 80% completion with 10K views > 15% completion with 100K views
- **AI-only content is being deprioritized** — algorithm detects pure AI slop
- BUT: AI-assisted content with human curation/editing is NOT penalized
- Educational niche content gets pushed harder than generic entertainment
- Interactive hooks (questions, cliffhangers, "wait for it") = more completion

### YouTube (Long-Form + Shorts)
- Shorts: "explore and exploit" model — retention >75% gets amplified
- Long-form: 70-82% retention is the sweet spot for faceless educational content
- Channel consistency > individual video performance
- High RPM niches get proportionally more advertiser budget pushed through
