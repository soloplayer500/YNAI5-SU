## DM Etiquette

- Do NOT cold DM members unless they've engaged with you first.
- Prefer #self-promo channels where they exist.
- If a mod calls you out: apologize, delete, leave. Don't argue.
- Offer value in general channels (answer questions, share charts) BEFORE dropping any link.
- Track: server name → post date → outcome (channel deleted? 0 clicks? 5 subs?). Iterate on winners.
