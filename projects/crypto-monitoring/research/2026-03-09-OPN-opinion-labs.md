## What It Is
Opinion is a **macro prediction market** on BNB Chain + Ethereum.
Users trade outcomes of real-world macroeconomic events (equity signals, financial data).
Settlement is oracle-based via Chainlink — no human decides outcomes, data feeds do.
Differentiator: macro/equity focus vs Polymarket (politics) and Kalshi (US-regulated events).
